What is Conquerors of The Craft?

Conquerors of The Craft is a popular Minecraft nations server where players can simulate civilizations, economies, geopolitics, and even wars. The original server was created on 13 June 2024 by Axader, and our community has grown tremendously since, and continues to grow today. Recently, on 16 May 2026, we launched the new and permanent Conquerors of The Craft 2 server. You can click the "Discord" link on the navbar to get more information on how to start playing our server and join our community!
